Enquire about a meeting room ❯

The meeting room within Kingfisher Barn is five metres by seven and a half metres in size, equipped with a projector, screen, whiteboard and flipchart and can accommodate approximately:

• 17 delegates horse-shoe style arrangement

• 20 delegates boardroom style arrangement

• 20 delegates cabaret style arrangement

• 28 delegates theatre style arrangement

The venue is available for bookings from Monday to Friday between 8am and 5.30pm (11am - 4pm during school holidays)
