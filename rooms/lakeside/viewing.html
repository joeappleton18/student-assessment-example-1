Lakeside Country Park’s Lakeside Centre offers a tranquil space in an architecturally stunning, waterside building. This flexible, affordable space is ideal for conferences, meetings, events, team-building activities, private parties and health and wellbeing activities.

It can accommodate up to 60 people theatre style.

The centre’s distinctive, modern design offers panoramic views from all our meeting rooms over the surrounding lakes.

We can cater for hot drinks and a range of traditional or luxury afternoon teas.

A flexible space

The Centre consists of two state-of the-art buildings, the main Centre and a service building, each with a distinctive, curved design. The Lakeside Centre can be hired as the whole room (Otter Room), or separated into two smaller rooms (Hedgehog or Harvest Mouse Rooms).

The Main Building offers:

• improved changing rooms

• a multi-purpose training and conference suite that can accommodate up to 60 people theatre style

• Changing Places facilities for disabled people with complex needs

• rentable office space

The Stores Building includes:

• eight storage rooms of varying sizes for hire

• a workshop

• public toilets
