Main Auditorium

The main auditorium offers flexible seating retractable circle seating, fixed balcony seating and two seated galleries, which allows you to either flat floor the space or offer tiered theatre seating for up to 306 delegates.

A range of state-of-the-art digital audio-visual equipment and professional digital theatre lighting and sound can be provided to meet your needs when using the theatre. This space is fully accessible.

The stage area is a maximum width of 8m, maximum depth of 10m, excluding wings with a maximum working height of 5.5m.

The auditorium measures 11m by 10m excluding stage.


Conference Meetings & Facilities

With our proximity to Eastleigh, Southampton, Winchester and Portsmouth, our building and facilities make an ideal conference facility. We are just 60 miles south west of London and easily accessible by public transport and road.

Our theatre benefits from a fully accessible, versatile auditorium space with a 306 fully seated capacity. The space comprises of flexible stalls seating, retractable circle seating, fixed balcony seating and two seated galleries.

Our Foyer and Bar space can be hired with the auditorium or separately and provide an ideal space for intimate performances, celebrations, formal receptions and more.

A range of state-of-the-art digital audio-visual equipment and professional digital theatre lighting and sound can be used to meet your needs when using the theatre.
