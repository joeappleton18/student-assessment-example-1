The Point’s creative environment and flexible spaces has seen it host international conferences, national events, regional networking and training events for organisations as diverse as the Police, Health Service, European Programme and the BBC’s Question Time.

Main Auditorium

The main auditorium offers flexible seating which allows you to either flat floor the space with tables and chairs for up to 110 or offer tiered theatre seating for up to 312 delegates, making it the perfect space for a conference or even a dinner and dance.

A range of audio visual equipment can be provided including projection capability and professional theatre lighting and sound can be used to meet your needs when using the theatre. This space is fully accessible.

The stage area is a maximum width of 12.5m, maximum depth of 10.1m, maximum proscenium opening of 10.4m, maximum working height of 6m.

The auditorium measures 13.5m by 15.5m.

The Studio Theatre

The intimate Studio Theatre can accommodate up to 50 people theatre style or 20 people classroom style. This space is perfect for meetings, presentations, receptions, smaller conferences and small, informal performances.

Wireless internet access is available in the Studio Theatre, as well as a range of audio visual equipment including projection capability, professional theatre lighting and sound. This space is also fully accessible and has ample natural light and a black-out facility.

Our wonderful Cafe Bar can provide various catering options to meet your requirements including refreshments, hot and cold buffets and canapés.

The Studio Theatre measures 8m by 8m.

The Conference Room

The Point’s bright and spacious Conference Room can accommodate up to 40 people in theatre style or 25 people in boardroom configuration.

Wireless internet access is available in the Conference Room, as well as a range of audio visual equipment including projection capability. This meeting room is also fully accessible and has ample natural light and a black-out facility. The Point also has a stunning roof terrace that overlooks the park, providing the perfect break-out area to the Conference Room and a delightful entertaining space.

Our wonderful Cafe Bar can provide various catering options to meet your requirements including refreshments, hot and cold buffets and canapés.

The Conference Room measures 6.5m by 6.5m.

The Irving Room

The comfortable Irving Room can accommodate meetings of up to 30 people in theatre style or 20 boardroom configuration.

Wireless internet access is available in the Irving Room, as well as a range of audio visual equipment including projection capability. This meeting room is also fully accessible and has ample natural light.

Our wonderful Cafe Bar can provide various catering options to meet your requirements including refreshments, hot and cold buffets and canapés.

The Irving Room measures 7.5m by 4.5m.
